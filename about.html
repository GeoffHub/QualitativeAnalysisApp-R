Exploratory Qualitative Analysis with R

Application Description:

This interactive application is intended to show how different statistical analyses of textual data could provide insights into the content of our data. This application does not intend to do away with thorough and interactive analyses in favour of basic, GUI-driven analyses. The techniques presented here may be found useful by users for some of their purposes, while in others they may deem the techniques insufficient. The objective of the author is more to provide a window into Qualitative analyses and to generate interest therein.

Aims:

The present application aims to allow users to import Textual Data, process it to remove any unnecessary data, and conduct the following exploratory analyses on the remaining, useful data:

  • Word Frequency Distributions (Rank-Frequency Plots)
  • Hierarchical Clustering for Words (Dendrograms)
  • Partitional Clustering for Words (Associative Word Clouds)
  • Partitional Clustering for Documents (Multidimensional Scaling and Topic Modeling)
  • Words' Associations (Words' Network)
