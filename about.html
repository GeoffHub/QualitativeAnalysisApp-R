Exploratory Qualitative Analysis with R

Application Description:

This interactive application was originally developed for the Office of the Police and Crime Commissioner, Norfolk, where it would be used to analyse Textual Data generated from surveys. In its present form, however, it has been modified to include a few additional functions that were not part of the original application.

Aims:

The present application aims to allow users to import Textual Data, process it to remove any unnecessary data, and conduct the following exploratory analyses on the remaining, useful data:

  • Word Frequency Distributions (Rank-Frequency Plots)
  • Hierarchical Clustering for Words (Dendrograms)
  • Partitional Clustering for Words (Associative Word Clouds)
  • Partitional Clustering for Documents (Distance matrices & Multi-dimensional Scaling)
  • Words' Associations (Words' Network)
Note: This application does not intend to do away with thorough and interactive analyses in favour of GUI-driven analyses. The techniques presented here may be found useful by users in some of their applications, while in others they may be deemed insufficient. The intent of the author is more to provide a window into Qualitative analyses and to generate interest.
